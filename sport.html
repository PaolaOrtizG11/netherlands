One of the most popular sports in the Netherlands, with a strong national presence and internationally successful men’s and women’s teams. Popularity and Culture Field hockey holds a prominent place in Dutch sports culture, especially among youth and women. It is widely played in schools, clubs, and local communities, with active participation from both amateur and professional levels. The sport is often celebrated for its organization, quality of training, and competitive domestic leagues. Domestic Competitions The top domestic league is the Hoofdklasse, considered one of the strongest field hockey leagues in the world. Both men’s and women’s Hoofdklasse leagues feature elite clubs that compete at high professional standards.
